About sheCodes

sheCodes is the ACM-W Chapter at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. We work to promote and support the growing community of women in computer science and technology. Our purpose is to support and promote women in CS by encouraging discussion and raising awareness regarding issues surrounding Women in Computer Science.

Get Involved with sheCodes!

There are a lot of ways for you to get involved. The easiest is to come to our events. We love to see new faces, and that way we can talk in person about how you can be more involved with sheCodes. If you have a specific way you'd like to get involved (like a project you want help with, or a study break idea you want to see executed) send an email to sheCodes.

We also have initiatives that are always looking for new members. There is Mentorship and FWD Conference. Read more and reach out to get involved. We're excited to have you.

What We Do

Mentorship Program

The mentorship program matches every Freshman and Sophomore woman in Computer Science with an older woman who is also studying CS. The mentor helps the mentee with class selection, internship searches, moral support, academic advice, and generally becomes a much-needed, loving guide to achieving success as a woman in CS. If you'd like to get involved with this program (as a mentor or a mentee), please email us.

Future Women Developers (FWD) Conference

Once a year, sheCodes invites middle/high school women to participate in a day filled with interactive workshops and fun activities to expose them to computer science. The Future Women Developers (FWD) program will give young women a taste of the tremendous creativity and innovation involved in computer science and engineering or science career, and show young women how these fields can help change the world. For more information, please visit the FWD site. If you'd like to get involved with FWD (as an leader or volunteer), please email us.

Socials

sheCodes wants to build and strengthen the growing community of women in CS at Cal Poly Pomona and raise awareness of the issues women in the field face. We would love for you to come out and join!

Company Tours

From time to time, sheCodes goes out as a club to tour local tech companes. If you'd like to learn more or join us, please email us.
